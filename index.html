About the ministry

Blom's Ministries is a welcoming faith community with a gentle countryside atmosphere, built around prayer, encouragement, healing, and time spent in God's presence.

What people can expect

Visitors can expect a peaceful environment, compassionate people, and a space where hearts can be restored, faith can be strengthened, and lives can be refreshed through Christ-centered ministry.

The heart behind Blom's Ministries

This ministry is for people carrying pain, questions, exhaustion, or a desire to grow deeper in faith. The vision is to offer a peaceful, Christ-centered country setting where people can talk, pray, reflect, heal, and be reminded that God still meets people personally.

“A place where you can come with the pain of the past, rest in the presence of your Heavenly Father, and choose healing that can bring hope to others.”

Prayer and encouragement

A welcoming ministry space for prayer, encouragement, and spiritual support for individuals, families, and anyone needing hope.

Messages and devotion
